Problem Statement

Craigslist, despite being a widely used classifieds platform, suffers from an outdated user interface, poor search functionality, and security concerns. Users face difficulties in navigating listings, verifying seller credibility, and avoiding scams. The lack of modern design elements and trust-building features results in a frustrating experience, reducing user engagement and transaction reliability.

Initial Research & Findings

To understand user issues, we conducted surveys, usability tests, and competitor analysis. Here's what we found:

✖ Hard to Navigate – Poor search filters make finding listings difficult.
✖ Lack of Trust & Security – No verification system leads to scams and fake listings.
✖ Outdated UI – The design is cluttered and not mobile-friendly.
✓ Better Alternatives Exist – Competitors like Facebook Marketplace offer secure payments and better filters.

Detailed Research Findings & Insights

Usability Testing Insights

1

Navigation Issues: Some users found navigation acceptable, but many struggled with finding specific categories like jobs and rentals.

2

Complex Ad Posting: Users found the process tedious due to too many steps and the lack of a "preview" feature before posting.

3

Limited Filtering Options: The absence of detailed filters made it difficult for users to refine their searches effectively.

Actionable Insights:

  • Improve category browsing for easier navigation
  • Add a "preview" option before posting ads
  • Enhance search filters to allow better customization

Survey Findings

1

Frequent Use: Users often visit Craigslist for job searches and buying/selling goods.

2

Mixed Satisfaction: Average rating: 3/5. Users appreciate variety but are frustrated by outdated design and poor filters.

3

Trust Issues: Users face challenges with fraudulent listings and unreliable sellers/landlords.

4

Desired Features: 90% of users requested better filters, scam protection, and a modernized interface.

Analytics Insights

1

High Engagement in Jobs & Rentals: Users frequently visit these sections but disengage due to poor navigation and communication issues.

2

Filtering Challenges: Many users waste time sorting through irrelevant results due to inadequate filters.

3

Trust & Security Concerns: Fake listings impact user confidence, especially in job and rental sections.
